## Introduction

The CodeWP platform is intended to be as simple as possible. It's designed to be used by anyone, regardless of their technical knowledge. With that said, there are many subfeatures, functions, and options that can be overwhelming at first. This guide will help you understand all aspects of the CodeWP platform UI/UX and how to use it.

We'll cover the following topics:

- Screens
- Navigation & buttons
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Settings

Offering examples, screenshots, and step-by-step instructions, this guide will help you get the most out of the CodeWP platform.
